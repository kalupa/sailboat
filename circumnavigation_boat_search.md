---

## Decision Framework

| Axis | What it means for selection |
|------|---------------------------|
| **Speed** | Westsail 32 ≈ 100 nm/day. Tayana 37 / PS 37 ≈ 120-130 nm/day. Valiant 40 ≈ 140-150 nm/day. A 4-year window (2033-2037) is tight for boats under 110 nm/day average. |
| **Refit status** | Fully refit turnkey at top of budget usually beats cheap project + DIY refit. Beth Leonard's 1:1 purchase-to-outfit ratio rule holds on 40+ year-old hulls. |
| **Proven design** | Top tier: Valiant 37/40, Pacific Seacraft 37. Second tier: Tayana 37, Hallberg-Rassy 36/37, Hans Christian 38, Westsail 32 (slow but proven). |
| **Location for learning phase** | Seattle/Anacortes <90 min from North Van = ~$5-10K convenience value over remote BC locations. |
| **Hard elimination criteria** | Center cockpit, Island Packet 35, ferro-cement, cored hull with water intrusion, fractional ownership. |
